Privacy Policy

Here Links · Locusable Studio · Effective July 24, 2026

Here Links (“the App”) is made by Locusable Studio (“we”, “us”). The App is an unofficial native iOS client for self-hosted linkding servers that you operate. We do not operate user accounts for the App, and we do not use data for tracking. We do not sell personal data.

What we don’t collect

We do not operate analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services that receive your information. We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for marketing. We do not run a Locusable backend that stores your bookmarks or browsing history.

Data that stays on your device

The App stores the following on your iPhone to provide its features. This data is not sent to us:

  • API tokens — stored in the Keychain so the App (and Share Extension) can authenticate to your linkding servers.
  • Server settings — instance URLs and related preferences stored in an App Group on your device for use by the App and Share Extension.

Your linkding servers

When you use the App, bookmark lists, searches, saves, archives, tags, snapshots, and attachments are requested from the linkding server URL(s) you configure. Those requests are made by the App on your device to your self-hosted instance(s). How that server stores or processes data is governed by your own hosting setup and the linkding project — not by Locusable Studio.

Share Extension

The optional Share Extension lets you save a link from other apps into your default linkding server. It uses the same on-device Keychain tokens and App Group settings as the main App.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the effective date above.

Contact

For questions about this policy or technical support, open an issue at github.com/locusable-studio/HereLinks/issues.
