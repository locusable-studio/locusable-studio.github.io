Privacy Policy

Here Wallpaper · Locusable Studio · Effective July 12, 2026

Here Wallpaper (“the App”) is made by Locusable Studio (“we”, “us”). The App may access limited data on your device to provide its features. We do not operate user accounts, and we do not use data for tracking. We do not sell personal data.

Location

The App may collect precise location only when you tap the locate control to use your current position.

  • Purpose — App Functionality: to generate a wallpaper for your current location.
  • Linked to your identity — No. The App has no accounts and does not associate location with an identity.
  • Used for tracking — No.

Location is processed on your device for creating the wallpaper. We do not run our own servers that store your location history.

What we don’t collect

We do not operate analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services that receive your information. We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for marketing.

Other data that stays on your device

The App may also use these system features on your iPhone or iPad. This data is processed on your device and is not sent to us:

  • Photo Library — optional, only when you save an exported wallpaper.
  • App settings — preferences such as theme and layout are stored locally on your device.

Purchases

Optional Pro subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your payment card details. Apple’s privacy practices apply to those transactions.

Map content

Map tiles and place labels may be loaded from map data providers (for example OpenFreeMap / OpenStreetMap-related services) so the App can display maps. Those requests are made by the App on your device to show map imagery; we do not run a separate backend that collects your identity from them.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the effective date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through App Store support for Here Wallpaper.
